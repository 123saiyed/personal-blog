Step 1 — Preview your LinkedIn card image below

This is exactly what people will see when you share your portal link on LinkedIn or WhatsApp.

Step 2 — Add it to your portal (one time only)

  1. Click the button above — og-image.png will download to your Downloads folder
  2. Move it into your project folder: C:\Users\MY PC\Desktop\personal\
  3. Open Git Bash / terminal in that folder and run:
    git add og-image.png && git commit -m "Add OG preview image" && git push
  4. Done! LinkedIn will now show the card preview when you share your link.
